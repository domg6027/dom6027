Paleo-Messianic

Short Covenant Prayers

These short prayers can be memorized for daily devotion, QARA, or whenever a prayer guide is unavailable. Each section groups prayers by circumstance.

Distress & Protection

"Elohim is our refuge and strength; a very present help in trouble."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 46:1

"Fear not, for I am with you; be not dismayed, for I am your Elohim."
- Yeshayahu (Isaiah) 41:10

"Be strong and courageous; do not fear, for YHVH your Elohim goes with you."
- Devarim (Deuteronomy) 31:6

"He who dwells in the shelter of the Most High will abide in the shadow of Shaddai."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 91:1

Thanksgiving & Praise

"I will bless YHVH at all times; His praise shall continually be in my mouth."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 34:1

"The Master is my shepherd; I shall not want."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 23:1

"YHVH is my light and my salvation; whom shall I fear?"
- Tehillim (Psalms) 27:1

"Give thanks to YHVH, for He is good; His mercy endures forever."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 136:1

Provision & Guidance

"Trust in YHVH with all your heart, and lean not on your own understanding."
- Mishlei (Proverbs) 3:5

"The Master is near to all who call upon Him."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 145:18

"Give Your servant an understanding heart to judge Your people and discern between good and evil."
- Melakhim Alef (1 Kings) 3:9

"I will instruct you and teach you the way you should go; I will guide you with My eye."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 32:8

Courage & Strength

"Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid or discouraged, for YHVH your Elohim is with you wherever you go."
- Devarim (Deuteronomy) 31:6

"Wait for YHVH; be strong and let your heart take courage; wait for YHVH!"
- Tehillim (Psalms) 27:14

"Do not fear, for I have redeemed you; I have called you by name, you are Mine."
- Yeshayahu (Isaiah) 43:1

"Do not be afraid, little flock, for your Father delights to give you the kingdom."
- Luke 12:32

Love & Compassion

"Love your neighbor as yourself."
- Vayikra (Leviticus) 19:18

"This is My commandment, that you love one another as I have loved you."
- Yochanan (John) 15:12

"Be kind to one another, tenderhearted, forgiving one another, as Elohim in Messiah forgave you."
- Efesiyim (Ephesians) 4:32

"Do justice, love kindness, and walk humbly with your Elohim."
- Michah (Micah) 6:8

Repentance & Renewal

"Create in me a clean heart, O Elohim, and renew a steadfast spirit within me."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 51:10

"Return to Me with all your heart, with fasting, weeping, and mourning."
- Yirmeyahu (Jeremiah) 4:8

"Elohim, be merciful to me, a sinner."
- Luke 18:13

"Wash me thoroughly from my iniquity and cleanse me from my sin."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 51:2

Messianic & Covenant Identity

"I will make you a great nation and bless you; all peoples on earth will be blessed through you."
- Bereshit (Genesis) 12:2–3

"The Master bless you and keep you; make His face shine upon you and be gracious; lift up His countenance upon you and give you peace."
- Bamidbar (Numbers) 6:24–26

"Hear, O Yisrael: YHVH our Elohim, YHVH is One. Love YHVH your Elohim with all your heart, soul, and strength."
- Devarim (Deuteronomy) 6:4–5

Holy Spirit & Guidance

"I will instruct you and teach you the way you should go; I will guide you with My eye."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 32:8

"The Spirit of the Master is upon Me, because He has anointed Me to proclaim good news to the poor."
- Yeshayahu (Isaiah) 61:1

"Likewise, the Spirit helps us in our weakness; for we do not know what we should pray for as we ought."
- Romiyim (Romans) 8:26

Justice & Righteousness

"Let justice roll down like waters, and righteousness like an ever-flowing stream."
- Amos 5:24

"Learn to do good; seek justice, correct oppression; bring justice to the fatherless, plead for the widow."
- Yeshayahu (Isaiah) 1:17

"Righteousness and justice are the foundation of Your throne."
- Tehillim (Psalms) 89:14

Shabbat & Covenant Remembrance

"Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy."
- Shemot (Exodus) 20:8

"Six days you shall labor and do all your work, but the seventh day is a Shabbat to YHVH your Elohim."
- Devarim (Deuteronomy) 5:12

"You shall observe the Shabbat, because it is holy to you."
- Vayikra (Leviticus) 23:3
