Justice - economic trust

🟫 MITZVOT FOCUS

YHVH calls His people to walk in justice, diligence, and trust rather than greed, exploitation, or dishonest gain. Economic trust is built through integrity, fairness, patience, and dependence upon His provision instead of manipulation or oppression.

True prosperity is not measured only by increase, but by righteousness, faithfulness, and obedience in how resources are gained and used.

🩵 REFLECTION

Are your financial decisions driven by trust in YHVH or by fear and striving?

Today is a call to pursue honest stewardship, to reject exploitation, and to build lives marked by integrity, generosity, and trust in His provision.

🌾 OMER 39

Today is Day 39 of the Omer. As we continue this journey toward Shavu'ot, we are reminded that trust and justice must shape every area of life, including our labor, finances, and responsibilities.

Each day drawing us deeper into discipline, faithfulness, and alignment with the ways of YHVH.

Today also mark the EVE of ASCENSION DAY which falls on Omer 40.

🕯️ PREPARATION FOR PESACH SHENI

As preparations continue for Pesach Sheni, let homes and hearts continue to be cleansed from leaven and compromise. Continue preparing unleavened bread, spices, and all things necessary for the coming remembrance and Seder observance where applicable.

Let these preparations also remind us to remove spiritual corruption, pride, and dishonesty, so that we may approach the appointed time with sincerity, humility, and thanksgiving before YHVH.

👐 PRAYER

Avinu Malkeinu, teach us to trust You in every area of provision and responsibility. Guard us from greed, fear, exploitation, and dishonest gain. Establish within us hearts of integrity, generosity, diligence, and contentment. As we prepare for Pesach Sheni, cleanse our hearts and homes, and help us to walk faithfully before You in righteousness and truth.

Baruch YHVH.

Aaronitic Blessing

Shalom until tomorrow.
