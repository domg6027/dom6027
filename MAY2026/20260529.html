📨 DAVAR LECHEM – Erev Shabbat, Shlishi 12, 6028 AA

Shalom, Mishpacha!

⭐ THEME

Justice – reliability of words

📘 READING

“Lying lips are an abomination to YHVH, but those who deal truly are His delight.”
- Mishlei (Proverbs) 12:22

“Let your word ‘Yes’ be ‘Yes,’ and your ‘No,’ ‘No.’ And what goes beyond these is from the wicked one.”
- Mattithyahu (Matthew) 5:37

🟫 MITZVOT FOCUS

YHVH calls His people to speak with honesty, consistency, and reliability. Words carry authority and responsibility. False promises, careless speech, exaggeration, deception, and manipulative communication weaken trust and damage relationships within the community.

Reliability of words reflects integrity of character. The people of YHVH are called to speak truthfully, fulfill commitments faithfully, and communicate with clarity and sincerity. Righteous speech strengthens justice, trust, and peace among people.

🩵 REFLECTION

In a world filled with misinformation, broken promises, and careless speech, reliability has become increasingly rare. Many people speak impulsively without considering the weight or consequences of their words. Yet Scripture teaches that words reveal the condition of the heart. A trustworthy person learns restraint, wisdom, and consistency in speech. Reliability is built through truthfulness practiced daily in both small and great matters.

Consider whether your words consistently reflect honesty, dependability, and integrity. A heart that walks closely with YHVH learns to value truth above convenience, image, or emotional reaction.

👐 PRAYER

Abba YHVH, teach us to walk in truth, integrity, and faithfulness. Guard our mouths from careless speech, exaggeration, deception, and empty promises. Help us to speak with wisdom, sincerity, and reliability in every circumstance. Strengthen us to honor our words and reflect righteousness in both speech and action. Let our lives bring peace, trust, and honor to Your Name.

We pray for peace in the Middle East, South Africa, and the world. Bring stability, righteousness, and divine intervention where conflict rises.

We pray for end-time revival. Let hearts awaken and turn back to Your truth across the earth.

We pray for the elderly, especially those suffering abuse or neglect. Surround them with protection, dignity, care, and comfort.

We pray for the farmers, especially those in danger of attacks. Shield their lives, strengthen their hands, and bless their labor.

We pray for evangelists across the world, especially where persecution is severe. Empower them with courage, safety, and supernatural doors of opportunity.

We pray for ill people, especially those with cancer and new viral outbreaks. Stretch out Your healing hand and bring restoration and hope.

We pray for the coming tribulation and the signs already appearing. Prepare our hearts with wisdom, endurance, and steadfast faith.

We pray for Derech Olam Mishkan Int'l. Guide each step, open the right doors, and let Your favor rest upon the work.

We pray for grieving families and all who have suffered through violence, terror, and injustice. Comfort the brokenhearted, surround them with Your nearness, and uphold them with peace and justice.

Baruch YHVH.
