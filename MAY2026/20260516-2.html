🌟 SHABBAT MESSAGE – Sheni 27, 6028 AA 🌟

Shalom, Mitspacha!

✨ THEME

Prohibition of delaying justice / Dwelling of holiness (Exodus 25–31)

👐 OPENING PRAYER

Avinu Malkeinu, teach us to act justly and without delay. Establish our hearts in Your holiness and guide us to dwell in Your presence with reverence and obedience. May our actions today reflect Your truth and righteousness, and may we never postpone the work of justice or the building of Your dwelling among us.

Baruch YHVH.

🍷 COMMUNION

As we partake in communion, let us remember Yahusha HaMashiach, our Pesach Lamb, whose obedience and sacrifice brought redemption. May this cup remind us to live faithfully, to pursue justice diligently, and to cultivate holiness in our hearts and dwellings.

📜 TORAH READING

"Build for Me a sanctuary, that I may dwell among them."
- Exodus 25:8

"You shall not pervert justice; you shall not show partiality; and you shall not accept a bribe, for a bribe blinds the eyes of the wise and subverts the cause of the righteous."
- Devarim (Deuteronomy) 16:19

"Learn to do good; seek justice, correct oppression; bring justice to the fatherless, plead the widow’s cause."
- Yeshayahu (Isaiah) 1:17

📖 MESSAGE

Today’s Shabbat invites us to reflect on the dual call of justice and holiness. Justice is not optional and must not be delayed—especially when others depend on us to act rightly. At the same time, YHVH calls His people to dwell in holiness, both in our hearts and in our community, following His detailed instructions from Exodus 25–31.

Delaying justice undermines covenant life; it allows oppression to fester and harms the fabric of trust among the people of YHVH. Dwelling in holiness, on the other hand, aligns our lives with the Divine presence and cultivates sanctity in all we do.

💙 APPLICATION

Examine areas in your life where justice has been postponed—whether in decisions, speech, or action. Align your home, work, and community with both fairness and reverence.

Let holiness guide your interactions, your words, and your stewardship, ensuring that each act honors YHVH and demonstrates integrity.

🌾 OMER 43

Today is Day 43 of the Omer. Each day continues to refine our discipline, accountability, and faithfulness.

As we dwell in holiness and pursue justice, let the count of the Omer remind us that spiritual refinement is a daily, deliberate practice. Every day of obedience and integrity draws us closer to alignment with YHVH’s will.

👐 CLOSING PRAYER

Avinu Malkeinu, help us to act without delay in matters of justice. Establish our hearts in holiness, guide our steps in righteousness, and empower us to dwell faithfully in Your presence. May our words, deeds, and decisions today reflect Your truth and bring honor to Your Name.

Baruch YHVH.
