📨 DAVAR LECHEM – Yom Chamishi, Sheni 18, 6028 AA

Shalom, Mishpacha!

⭐ THEME

Prohibition of stealing by deception

📘 READING

"Dishonest scales are detestable to YHVH, but accurate weights find favor with Him."
- Mishlei (Proverbs) 11:1

"Do not lie to each other, since you have taken off your old self with its practices."
- Colossians 3:9

"The righteous hate what is false, but the wicked make themselves a stench and bring shame on themselves."
- Mishlei (Proverbs) 13:5

🟫 MITZVOT FOCUS

We are commanded not only to avoid physical theft, but also every form of deception that unjustly takes from another. False weights, dishonest speech, manipulation, and hidden motives are all forms of stealing through deceit. YHVH desires honesty, transparency, and integrity in all our dealings.

Truthfulness protects trust. Integrity preserves righteousness. Deception corrupts both the heart and the community.

🩵 REFLECTION

Are there areas in your life where honesty has been compromised, even in small ways?

Today is a call to walk in complete integrity - to reject deception and to deal truthfully with others in every matter.

🌾 OMER 34

Today is Day 34 of the Omer. As we continue this journey of refinement, we are reminded that integrity and truthfulness guard us from hidden corruption.

Each day drawing us deeper into honesty, righteousness, and alignment with YHVH.

👐 PRAYER

Avinu Malkeinu, search our hearts and expose every trace of deception within us. Teach us to walk in honesty and integrity in all things. Guard our speech, our actions, and our intentions from falsehood and manipulation. Establish within us a spirit that loves truth and reflects Your righteousness in every matter.

Baruch YHVH.

Aaronitic Blessing

Shalom until tomorrow.
