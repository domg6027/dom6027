📰 DAVAR LECHEM – Yom Sheni, Sheni 22, 6028 AA

Shalom, Mishpacha!

⭐ THEME

Accuracy in weights and measures

📘 READING

"Use honest scales, honest weights, a full ephah, and a full hin. Do not cheat in buying or selling."
- Devarim (Deuteronomy) 25:15

"Dishonest scales are an abomination to YHVH, but a just weight is His delight."
- Mishlei (Proverbs) 11:1

"One who is faithful in little will also be faithful in much, and one who is dishonest in little will be dishonest in much."
- Luke 16:10

"Bring justice to the fatherless; plead the widow’s cause."
- Yeshayahu (Isaiah) 1:17

"Whatever you do, do it all for the glory of YHVH."
- 1 Corinthians 10:31

🟫 MITZVOT FOCUS

YHVH commands precise honesty in all measures, whether in commerce, daily trade, or personal dealings. Using false scales, underfilling, or exaggeration undermines trust and violates Torah principles.

Integrity is the standard of obedience and covenantal life.

🩵 REFLECTION

Examine your daily actions and transactions. Where might accuracy and honesty be compromised?

Today challenges us to live with integrity, measuring not only material things but also our words, promises, and judgments.

🌾 OMER 38

Today is Day 38 of the Omer. Each day calls us to refinement, discipline, and accountability. Accurate measures remind us that spiritual and practical integrity must align.

As we draw closer to Shavu'ot even our literal and spiritual harvests are busy ripening as we speak.

🕯️ PREPARATION FOR PESACH SHENI

As we approach Pesach Sheni, for those who are preparing for it, let us prepare both our hearts and homes. Remove all leaven from your houses, cleanse your spaces, and ready your hearts in sincerity and devotion. Begin baking unleavened bread, gather spices, and prepare the lamb for the Seder where applicable.

These next two to three days are for practical and spiritual preparation to enter the appointed remembrance with reverence and joy.

👐 PRAYER

Avinu Malkeinu, teach us to act with accuracy and integrity. Guide our hands, hearts, and minds to honor You in every measurement, word, and deed. Help us prepare faithfully for Pesach Sheni, cleansing ourselves, our homes, and our hearts, that we may celebrate this appointed time with sincerity, obedience, and joy.

Baruch YHVH.

Aaronitic Blessing

Shalom until tomorrow.
