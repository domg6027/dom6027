ABOUT US

Derech Olam Ministries Int'l is an online ministry adhering to the Torah and the original complete Bible.

WHAT DO WE BELIEVE?

We believe:

  1. God manifests as Father (YHVH – pronounced Yehuvah), Son (Yahusha HaMashiach), and Holy Spirit (Ru'ach HaQodesh). These are THREE distinct entities with ONE purpose. The Head is the Father (YHVH), and He alone is worshiped.
  2. The original scriptures, including the Apocrypha, are the highest authority.
  3. Baptism of believers is by immersion. Circumcision is required for all male believers, performed on the 8th day for boys born to believing parents.
  4. The baptism of the Holy Spirit (Ru'ach HaQodesh) empowers believers to be witnesses.
  5. The Holy Spirit (Ru'ach HaQodesh) resides within believers and equips them.
  6. The gifts of the Holy Spirit (Ru'ach HaQodesh) are active and valid today.
  7. The five-fold ministry (apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors, and teachers) is for the perfecting of the saints.
  8. Prayer is essential in our walk with YHVH.
  9. Yahusha died on the STAKE and was resurrected by YHVH. His death replaced all animal offerings ordained by YHVH. The stake—not the cross—was the Roman execution device.
  10. The End Times are unfolding as revealed in Scripture.
  11. Believers will participate in the resurrection, the Marriage Supper of the Lamb, and receive rewards at the Bema Seat of Yahusha HaMashiach. Unbelievers face judgment according to Scripture.
  12. We retain the Jewish lifestyle and follow the original calendar recorded by Mosheh. The Torah contains commandments to be obeyed.
  13. We accept the Bible in its ORIGINAL form, including the Apocrypha and Tanakh.
  14. We follow the kosher diet ordained by YHVH.
  15. Married women are to cover their heads during prayer; unmarried women may, but are not required outside prayer.
  16. The Shabbat is a day of rest, ordained from creation. Fridays are preparation days. Biblical days run sunset to sunset.
  17. The new month is determined by crescent moon observation, with adjustments as prescribed—especially in Tishrei.
  18. Days and months are numbered according to Hebrew tradition, with occasional realignment months before Pesach.
  19. The creational calendar counts from Adam. We are in the year 6027 AA (After Adam), starting at nightfall on March 30, 2025.

VISION

To call people to accept Yahusha as the Messiah and Ben Elohim, and to serve YHVH by His instructions and commandments as detailed in the Torah.

MISSION

To fulfill the commission given by Yahusha Messiah in Matthew 28:19–20 and related passages.

So wherever you go, make disciples of all nations. Baptize them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Teach them to do everything I have commanded you. And remember, I am with you until the end of time.
