📨 DAVAR LECHEM, Erev Shabbat, Shlishi 25, 6028 AA

Shalom, Mitspacha!

⭐ THEME

Justice – foundations of governance

📘 READING

“Righteousness exalts a nation, but sin is a reproach to any people.”
- Mishlei (Proverbs) 14:34

“By me sovereigns reign, and rulers make righteous inscriptions. By me princes rule, and nobles, all the judges of the earth.”
- Mishlei (Proverbs) 8:15-16

🟫 MITZVOT FOCUS

YHVH establishes justice as one of the foundational pillars of righteous governance. Nations, communities, and leaders flourish when truth, integrity, accountability, and fairness guide decision-making. When justice is neglected, corruption, oppression, and instability increase.

Scripture teaches that authority and governance ultimately fall under the sovereignty of YHVH. Leaders are therefore accountable to uphold righteousness and to govern with wisdom, humility, and responsibility. The people of YHVH are likewise called to value justice and to support what is righteous and truthful.

🩵 REFLECTION

Throughout history, societies have either been strengthened or weakened according to the moral foundations upon which they were built. Governance without righteousness eventually produces disorder and suffering, while justice and integrity create stability and protection for communities.

Consider the role that truth, fairness, and accountability play within your own life and environment. Every believer contributes to the moral condition of society through daily conduct, decisions, and relationships. Honoring righteousness in small matters helps establish stronger foundations for families, communities, and future generations.

👐 PRAYER

Abba YHVH, thank You for being the source of justice, wisdom, and righteous authority. Teach leaders, judges, and rulers to govern with integrity, humility, and truth. Guard nations and communities from corruption, injustice, violence, and oppression. Help us also to walk in righteousness and to reflect Your character in our daily lives, decisions, and relationships.

We pray for peace in the Middle East, South Africa, and the world. Bring stability, righteousness, and divine intervention where conflict rises.

We pray for end-time revival. Let hearts awaken and turn back to Your truth across the earth.

We pray for the elderly, especially those suffering abuse or neglect. Surround them with protection, dignity, care, and comfort.

We pray for the farmers, especially those in danger of attacks. Shield their lives, strengthen their hands, and bless their labor.

We pray for people in war and disaster stricken areas. Comfort those who lost loved ones and heal those who have been injured.

We pray for evangelists across the world, especially where persecution is severe. Empower them with courage, safety, and supernatural doors of opportunity.

We pray for ill people, especially those with cancer and new viral outbreaks. Stretch out Your healing hand and bring restoration and hope.

We pray for the coming tribulation and the signs already appearing. Prepare our hearts with wisdom, endurance, and steadfast faith.

We pray for our ministry, Derech Olam Ministries. Guide each step, open the right doors, and let Your favor rest upon the work.

Baruch YHVH. Emet.

Shalom until tomorrow.
