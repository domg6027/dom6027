Why the Name Changed

Mishkan International Ministries Int'l has officially transitioned to Mishkan International.

This change reflects a continued return to the Hebrew roots and ancient paths of the faith once given to the forefathers, the prophets, and the early followers of Messiah.

The word Mishkan (משכן) means dwelling place, tabernacle, or place of presence.

In Scripture, the Mishkan was the set-apart dwelling where the Presence of YHVH resided among His people — a place of order, covenant, instruction, and reverence.

This name reflects the direction of this work: returning to the Ancient Path, the Torah of YHVH, the Renewed Covenant in Messiah, and the restoration of truth beyond man-made systems.

While the name has changed, the mission, teachings, daily Davar Lechem messages, and calling remain unchanged.

🌿 The Ancient Path Continues

Mishkan International remains dedicated to teaching, warning, encouraging, and preparing hearts for the times ahead through Scripture, covenant restoration, and daily instruction.

We continue to welcome all sincere seekers who desire truth and the narrow path that leads to life.
