Why the Name Changed

Derech Olam Ministries Int'l has officially transitioned to Derech Olam Mishkan International.

This change reflects a continued return to the Hebrew roots and ancient paths of the faith once given to the forefathers, the prophets, and the early followers of Messiah.

The word “Mishkan” (משכן) means dwelling place, tabernacle, or place of presence.

In the Scriptures, the Mishkan was the set-apart place where the Presence of YHVH dwelled among His people — a place of instruction, order, covenant, and reverence.

This name more accurately reflects the direction and purpose of this work: calling seekers back to the Ancient Path, the Torah of YHVH, the Renewed Covenant of Yahusha the Messiah, and the restoration of truth beyond man-made traditions and religious systems.

While the name has changed, the mission, teachings, daily Davar Lechem messages, and calling remain the same.

🌿 The Ancient Path Continues

Derech Olam Mishkan International remains dedicated to teaching, warning, encouraging, and preparing hearts for the times ahead through daily teachings, Scripture study, restoration of the covenant ways, and returning to the appointed paths of YHVH.

We continue to welcome all sincere seekers who hunger for truth and desire to walk the narrow and ancient way that leads to life.
