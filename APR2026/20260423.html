📨 DAVAR LECHEM - Yom Chamishi - Sheni 4, 6028 AA

Shalom, Mitspacha!

⭐ THEMES

Justice - Protection of the Household

📘 READING

“Unless YHVH builds the house, they labor in vain who build it; unless YHVH guards the city, the watchman stays awake in vain.”
- Tehillim (Psalms) 127:1

“You shall teach them diligently to your children, and shall talk of them when you sit in your house, when you walk by the way, when you lie down, and when you rise up.”
- Devarim (Deuteronomy) 6:7

🟫 MITZVOT FOCUS

Justice within the household begins with protection. A household that is guarded with care establishes a place of safety, instruction, and order. Protection is not limited to physical safety, but includes guarding values, behavior, and the atmosphere within the home. What is protected consistently becomes stable over time.

Those who lead within a household carry responsibility for maintaining structure. Teaching, correcting, and guiding are daily tasks that preserve stability. Justice is practiced when fairness, discipline, and consistency are maintained, ensuring that each member of the household understands responsibility and respect.

🩵 REFLECTION

Sheni 4 continues the strengthening of household structure. Protection does not happen by chance but through attentive and deliberate effort. When instruction is given regularly and expectations remain clear, confusion decreases and stability grows.

Guarding the household includes protecting time, speech, and conduct. What enters the home through words, actions, and influences shapes the character of those within it. Careful attention to these matters strengthens unity and builds resilience.

This day reminds us that protection is an ongoing responsibility. A household that is guided with discipline and fairness becomes a place where trust develops and responsibility is learned. Justice expressed through protection strengthens the household for future generations.

🌾 OMER 20

Twenty days have been counted. The fourth week begins, marking a shift from establishing patterns to strengthening endurance. Stability becomes more visible as disciplined routines continue to take shape.

Each counted day adds strength to the structure being built. What is protected and maintained today becomes the support for tomorrow.

👐 PRAYER

Blessed are You, YHVH our Elohim, King of the universe, who establishes households and teaches us to guard what has been entrusted to us. Thank You for the responsibility of guiding and protecting those within our care. Strengthen our ability to maintain order, discipline, and fairness within our homes.

Help us to guard our households with wisdom and attentiveness. Teach us to protect what is good, to correct what is harmful, and to build environments where stability and trust may grow.

Baruch YHVH.

Aaronitic Blessing

Shalom until tomorrow.
