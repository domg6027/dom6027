📨 DAVAR LECHEM - Yom Rishon, Rishon 23, 6028 AA

Shalom, Mitspacha!

⭐ THEME

Commit Your Works to YHVH — Establishing the Path Through Faithful Action

📘 READING

“Commit your works to YHVH, and your thoughts will be established.”
- Mishlei (Proverbs) 16:3

🟫 MITZVOT FOCUS

Faithfulness is strengthened through consistent action. Today’s focus is to align daily tasks with obedience, ensuring that even ordinary responsibilities are carried out with discipline and intention.

By committing each action to YHVH, the path becomes clearer and steadier.

🩵 REFLECTION

After the completion of Pesach and the transition through Havdalah, the rhythm of ordinary days returns. Yet ordinary does not mean without purpose. Each action taken today becomes part of the structure that builds endurance for the journey ahead. Commitment is not shown in single moments but in repeated faithfulness. Just as Yisrael continued step by step after crossing the waters, we also move forward through steady obedience.

When work is entrusted to YHVH, thoughts become ordered, and direction becomes firm.

🌾 OMER 09 — Strengthening the Foundation of the Second Cycle

Today marks the ninth day of counting the Omer. The first full week has been completed, and the second cycle has begun. This stage reminds us that growth comes through repetition and strengthening what has already been established.

The journey now moves beyond beginnings into endurance. Each new count reinforces discipline and steadiness. What started in deliverance now continues in structured obedience, forming the foundation needed for the days that lie ahead.

👐 PRAYER

Blessed are You, YHVH our Elohim, King of the universe, who grants us strength for each new day and establishes our steps through faithful obedience. Help us to commit our works to You and to walk in discipline as the counting continues. Strengthen our hands for the tasks before us and guide our thoughts so that our path remains steady and purposeful.

Baruch YHVH.

Aaronitic Blessing

Shalom until tomorrow.
